# Fast Physics-Based Modeling of Knots and Ties Using Templates

**Authors:** Dewen Guo, Zhendong Wang, Zegao Liu, Sheng Li, Guoping Wang, Yin Yang, Huamin Wang (Peking University, Style3D Research, University of Utah)  
**Publication:** SIGGRAPH 2025  
**Date:** 2025  
**Domain:** Computer Graphics / Cloth Simulation / Physics

---

## Abstract

Physics-based knot modeling using **pipe-like parametric knot templates** with Bézier curves.

- **Key:** Bézier curve as medial axis
- **Adaptively adjustable radius** for flexibility
- **Penetration-free** through collision detection
